The purpose of this website is to provide information about popular street food in Chicago. The site will help visitors discover different types of street food, learn about food trucks, and find affordable places to eat around the city.

The website aims to help people easily explore street food options in Chicago and learn more about the city’s food culture. It will provide helpful information such as food descriptions, locations, prices, and recommendations.

The intended audience includes tourists visiting Chicago, local residents who enjoy street food, college students looking for affordable meals, and anyone interested in trying new food experiences.

Many people do not know where to find the best street food or food trucks in Chicago. This website addresses that problem by organizing helpful information in one place. It gives users recommendations, food price comparisons, and locations of popular street food vendors.

The website will include written descriptions of different foods, images of street food and food trucks, lists of recommended places to eat, and information about local food events. It will also include a table comparing food prices and a contact form where visitors can send feedback or suggestions.

I chose these websites because they provide helpful information about food and restaurants in Chicago. They are well organized and visually appealing, which makes it easy for users to find information. These websites will help inspire the layout and content organization for my own website.
