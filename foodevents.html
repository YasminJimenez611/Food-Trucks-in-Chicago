Top Chicago Food Truck Festivals (2026 Season)

Chicago food truck events and festivals are headlined by the Chicago Food Truck Festival (June 20-21, 2026, in the South Loop) and weekly Chi Food Truck Fests at Daley Plaza (May–Oct).

hese events feature curated, gourmet mobile eats, live music, and outdoor dining. Key 2026 events include the Oak Forest Food Truck Festival and West Chicago Food Festival.

Chicago Food Truck Festival - Season 13 (June 20-21, 2026): Located at 2300 S. Indiana Ave, this major event features gourmet trucks, live music, and "Summer Daisy Margaritas".

Chicago Food Truck Fest at Daley Plaza (Fridays, May 15 – Oct 2, 2026): Located at 50 W. Washington Street, this event runs from 11 AM to 3 PM, offering a variety of food trucks in the heart of downtown.

Oak Forest Food Truck Festival (Check local schedules): Held at Chicago Gaelic Park, featuring diverse options like Happy Lobster, Grumpy Gaucho, and My Funnel Truck, with a beer garden and live music.

West Chicago Food Festival (Aug 22, 2026): An evening event (4-8 PM) in historic downtown West Chicago featuring food trucks, live music, and family activities.
